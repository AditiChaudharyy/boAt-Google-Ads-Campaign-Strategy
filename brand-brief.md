Campaign Opportunity 
boAt is currently under-investing in Google Ads, with only 82 paid keywords compared to Noise's 112. Despite generating organic traffic nearly three times higher than its competitors, its paid search presence remains relatively weak.
A well-structured Google Ads strategy focused on high-intent purchase keywords such as "best earphones under 1000" and "buy wireless earphones India" can significantly improve visibility among ready-to-buy customers and potentially capture 10–15% additional market share.
Additionally, Sony India has minimal paid search activity, leaving several high-intent commercial keywords largely uncontested and creating a valuable opportunity for boAt to strengthen its position in search advertising.

Peak Campaign Window 
Reason: This period includes major shopping festivals such as Navratri, Dussehra, Diwali, and Children's Day, leading to a significant increase in consumer purchase intent.
Google Trends Insight: boAt's search interest consistently reaches its annual peak of 100 during October, indicating maximum online demand.
Recommended Action: Increase the Google Ads budget by 35% during this period to maximize visibility, traffic, and conversions.
Secondary Campaign Window: January – February, driven by New Year purchases and Republic Day sales, offering an additional opportunity to boost campaign performance.
